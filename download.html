Get Allotropy

Allotropy is available from the Maven repository.

Allotropy requires Java 21+.

Download

  • allotropy-0.0.1.jar
  • allotropy-0.0.1-javadoc.jar
  • allotropy-0.0.1-sources.jar

</dependency>
